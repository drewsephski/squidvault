# Secure Video Sharing for Therapists: 5 Methods Compared

Sharing therapy session recordings requires balancing security, convenience, and compliance. Here's how five common methods stack up.

## Method 1: Email (Don't)

**The process**: Attach video file to email, hit send.

**Why it fails**:
- Email is not encrypted by default
- Attachments get stored on multiple servers
- No control over forwarding
- Zero audit trail

**Compliance status**: ❌ Not HIPAA-compliant

**Verdict**: Never use email for session videos.

## Method 2: Google Drive / Dropbox

**The process**: Upload to cloud storage, share password-protected link.

**Why it's problematic**:
- Personal accounts: No Business Associate Agreement (BAA)
- Google/Dropbox can technically access your files
- "Password protection" doesn't encrypt the file
- Limited audit logs (link clicked ≠ video viewed)

**Compliance status**: ⚠️ Requires business/enterprise plan with BAA

**Verdict**: Expensive and still has gaps.

## Method 3: Encrypted USB Drives

**The process**: Save to encrypted drive, mail or hand-deliver.

**Why it's secure but painful**:
- ✅ Truly encrypted and offline
- ❌ Physical logistics (shipping, meeting)
- ❌ No view confirmation
- ❌ Drive can be lost or damaged
- ❌ No instant revocation

**Compliance status**: ✅ Secure but impractical

**Verdict**: Works for annual archives, not weekly supervision.

## Method 4: Practice Management Platforms

**The process**: Use built-in features from SimplePractice, TherapyNotes, etc.

**Why it's expensive**:
- Integrated with your workflow
- HIPAA-compliant infrastructure
- ❌ Limited to their feature set
- ❌ No view receipts or detailed audit trails
- ❌ Expensive per-seat pricing

**Compliance status**: ✅ Generally compliant

**Verdict**: Good if you already use them, but specialized tools add features they won't.

## Method 5: Zero-Knowledge Video Vaults

**The process**: Upload, client-side encrypt, share password-protected link with expiration and view limits.

**Why it's the sweet spot**:
- ✅ Encryption happens in your browser (server never sees content)
- ✅ View receipts prove supervisor watched
- ✅ Automatic expiration and revocation
- ✅ Affordable ($10-15/month)
- ✅ Purpose-built for supervision

**Compliance status**: ✅ HIPAA-aligned with proper implementation

**Verdict**: Best balance of security, convenience, and price.
